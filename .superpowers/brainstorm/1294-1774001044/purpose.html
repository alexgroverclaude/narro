What should the public website be?

This shapes everything — Supabase usage, page structure, and what we build

A

Marketing landing page + waitlist

New homepage sells Narro as a product — hero, features, screenshots, email signup form. Supabase stores waitlist emails. Demo app stays at /narro. Screenshot generator moves to /screenshots. Best for: launching Narro publicly and collecting early users.

B

Deploy what exists + real data from Supabase

Keep the same pages but deploy to Vercel and replace hardcoded restaurant/deal/review data with real rows from Supabase. The demo app becomes a live demo with editable content. Best for: a working prototype you can show to investors or early testers.

C

Full product: landing page + live app + real data

Landing page at / with waitlist, the Narro demo app powered by real Supabase data, and the screenshot generator as a bonus tool. Supabase handles email signups, restaurants, deals, and reviews. Best for: shipping the full vision as a real website.
